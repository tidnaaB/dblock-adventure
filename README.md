## Introduction

"A D-Block Adventure" is an HTML, CSS, and JavaScript-based RPG game where the player takes on the role of a character trying to defeat Jadakiss, Styles P, and Sheek Looch â€” the originators of The LOX.

## Game Mechanics

* **XP, Health, and Cash:** Track your progress by managing your XP, health, and cash values.
* **Locations:** Explore various locations like the Bodega, the Streets, and more.
* **Enemies:** Battle opponents such as 'Bum,' 'Street Veteran,' and 'The LOX.'
* **Inventory:** Upgrade your weapons and manage your resources.
* **Win Conditions:** Defeat The LOX to win the game. Lose if your health reaches zero.

## Usage

Once you've loaded the game:

* Use the buttons to navigate through different locations.
* Fight enemies to gain XP and cash.
* Upgrade your health and weapons at the Bodega.
* Defeat The LOX to win the game.

## Technologies Used

* **HTML:** Structure of the web page.
* **CSS:** Styling for the game, ensuring a responsive and appealing design.
* **JavaScript:** Implements the game logic, including interactions, fighting mechanics, and navigation.
